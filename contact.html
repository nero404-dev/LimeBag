Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

+

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. Alternatively, you can use the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be returned in their original condition and packaging. Please visit our Returns page for more detailed information and to initiate a return.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship to over 25 countries worldwide. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. You can calculate shipping costs by adding items to your cart and entering your shipping address at checkout.

How do you ensure products are eco-friendly?

+

We have a rigorous vetting process for all products sold on LimeBag. Our sustainability team evaluates each item based on materials, production methods, packaging, and the supplier's environmental practices. We also prioritize products with recognized eco-certifications.
