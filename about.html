Our Story

How It All Started

LimeBag was founded in 2020 by a group of environmental enthusiasts who saw a need for a dedicated marketplace that makes sustainable products accessible and affordable.

What began as a small online store with just a few eco-friendly products has grown into a thriving marketplace featuring hundreds of sustainable options from around the world.

We believe that small changes in everyday purchasing decisions can lead to a significant positive impact on our planet. Our mission is to make these eco-friendly choices easier for consumers by bringing together the best sustainable products in one place.

Today, LimeBag serves thousands of environmentally conscious customers and partners with over 150 ethical producers and suppliers who share our commitment to sustainability and ethical business practices.
